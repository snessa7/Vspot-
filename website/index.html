Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vspot compatible with my Mac?

+

Vspot requires macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later. It works perfectly on both Intel and Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3) Macs.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

+

No! All your data stays 100% local on your machine. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your clipboard content or prompts. Your privacy is our priority.

What makes Vspot different from other clipboard managers?

+

Vspot is the ONLY clipboard manager with built-in AI prompt organization. While other tools just track your clipboard history, Vspot helps you organize, categorize, and instantly access your AI prompts with custom categories, tags, and lightning-fast search.

Is it a one-time purchase or subscription?

+

One-time purchase of $3.99. No subscriptions, no hidden fees. Buy once, own forever with free lifetime updates.

How much storage does Vspot use?

+

Vspot is incredibly lightweight, typically using less than 50MB of disk space. Your clipboard history and prompts are stored efficiently in a local database.

Can I sync Vspot across multiple Macs?

+

Currently, Vspot stores data locally for privacy. Multi-device sync via iCloud is planned for a future update based on user feedback.

Does Vspot work with all AI tools?

+

Yes! Vspot works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, and any other AI tool. It manages your clipboard content universally across all applications.

Is there a free trial?
